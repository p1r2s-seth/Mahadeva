About Us

Mahadeva Logistics and Warehousing is the leading integrated temperature-controlled logistics service provider operating on a pan-India level. Our temperature-controlled warehouses are set up adjacent to cities with a large potential for sourcing and/or consumption of temperature-sensitive products.

  • End-to-End Solutions

    We offer end-to-end logistics solutions covering the entire spectrum of services, from cold chain storage and warehousing to trucking and packaging services.

  • Quality control

    We prioritize quality control by implementing rigorous measures and protocols to keep your products fresh.

  • Value-added services

    We also offer value-added services such as product packaging, labeling, and customization to provide a comprehensive logistics solution that meets your unique needs.

Logistics

At Mahadeva, we offer end-to-end logistics solutions that are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our logistics services are designed to ensure the safe and efficient movement of goods, from the point of origin to the point of consumption. With our data-driven approach, we can identify opportunities to optimize your logistics operations and drive cost savings.

Warehousing

At Mahadeva, we offer comprehensive and customizable warehousing solutions to help businesses of all sizes manage their inventory and streamline their supply chain operations. Our state-of-the-art warehouses are strategically located across India, providing you with convenient access to major transportation routes and hubs. We offer a wide range of value-added services, including temperature-controlled storage, order fulfillment, inventory management, and cross-docking, to help you optimize your operations and reduce costs.
