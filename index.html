About Us

Mahadeva Logistics and Warehousing is the leading integrated temperature-controlled logistics service provider operating on a pan-India level. Our temperature-controlled warehouses are set up adjacent to cities with a large potential for sourcing and/or consumption of temperature-sensitive products.

  • End-to-End Solutions

    We offer end-to-end logistics solutions covering the entire spectrum of services, from cold chain storage and warehousing to trucking and packaging services.

  • Quality control

    We prioritize quality control by implementing rigorous measures and protocols to keep your products fresh.

  • Value-added services

    We also offer value-added services such as product packaging, labeling, and customization to provide a comprehensive logistics solution that meets your unique needs.
